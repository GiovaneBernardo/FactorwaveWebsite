About the Game

Factorwave is a fast-paced factory builder about scaling production while surviving pressure from a hostile planet. Players automate resource chains, protect critical infrastructure, and push their territory outward through Sector Hubs that unlock powerful bonuses.

The game blends factory planning with active defense and map control, giving players a compact loop of building, optimizing, expanding, and holding the line.

Key Features

Automate Production

Build and refine production systems that keep resources moving under pressure.

Expand Territory

Claim new ground and use Sector Hub bonuses to shape each run's strategy.

Defend Infrastructure

Protect factories, routes, and hubs from threats across a hostile planet.
